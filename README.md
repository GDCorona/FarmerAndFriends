## 🎮 About the Game
The farmer is stranded at the riverbank with a bag of beans and his two pets: a goose and a fox. The goose and the fox are both impaired and incapable of swimming. Your task is to help the farmer bring everything safely to the other side of the river using his small boat. 

It sounds simple, but you have to plan your moves carefully to make sure nobody gets eaten!

### ✨ Features
* **Classic Logic Puzzle:** Test your brain with the timeless river-crossing riddle.
* **Cozy Aesthetic:** Beautiful custom artwork, UI, and smooth screen transitions.
* **Hard Mode:** Looking for a challenge? Turn on Hard Mode to race against a 30-second clock with only 3 hearts (lives) to spare!
* **Immersive Audio:** Complete with background music and interactive sound effects.
* **Standalone Executable:** Fully compiled for Windows so anyone can play without installing Python.

## 📜 How to Play
1. The farmer is weak, so he can only take **one item** with him each time he crosses the river.
2. The fox and the goose don't get along. They **must not be left alone** without the farmer's supervision.
3. The goose is starving and will immediately **swallow all the beans** if left alone with them.
4. Click the buttons to move characters back and forth. Get everyone to the right side of the screen to win!
